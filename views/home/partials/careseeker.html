Care Seekers

Humanlink is a mobile and desktop app that enables compassionate groups in the caring process for individuals who are terminally ill or chronically ill or disabled.

The Humanlink platform nurtures dignity and independence for the patients and their families so that they don't feel overwhelmed by their caregiving needs.

Group Messaging

Caring for someone with a serious and complex illness is hard. Our group messaging app can help coordinate care between family members, volunteers and professional care providers.

General or private channels

Notebooks, emails, phone calls and loose notes get in the way of good care coordination. Our app makes it easy for you to save and share information between your caregiving group.

On Humanlink you can create general channels to share care needs with the group. You can also create private channels for sensitive conversations between family members and volunteers.
