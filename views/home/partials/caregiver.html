Care Professionals

Humanlink is a mobile and desktop app that enables compassionate groups in the caring process for individuals who are terminally ill or chronically ill or disabled.

The Humanlink platform nurtures dignity and independence for the patients and their families so that they don't feel overwhelmed by their caregiving needs.
