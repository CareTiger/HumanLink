  • Social Proofing: Identify the most trusted caregivers by reviewing their connections and the recommendations received from clients as well as connections.
  • Worry free back up care: Maintain a collection of vetted caregivers. When the primary caregiver backs out due to unavoidable reasons, the system will allow you to send out a beacon out to your collection of caregivers .
